Rootbeer Float

Rootbeer Float - Description

The rootbeer float is a traditional American dessert created in Cow Mountain, Colorado in 1893. The Rootbeer float consists of a root beer base and a floating vanilla ice cream scoop on top of it.

Rootbeer floats are quick and easy to prepare and make for a great dessert during the summer. Floats are very rich and creamy with a ginger-like aftertaste.

Rootbeer Float - Ingredients

  • 1 glass of root beer of your choosing
  • 1 scoop of vanilla bean ice cream

Rootbeer Float - Steps

  1. Fill an 8.0z glass with root beer.
  2. Top with one scroop of vanilla ice cream.
  3. Enjoy!
